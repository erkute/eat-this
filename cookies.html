What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

EAT THIS uses minimal cookies, primarily from third-party services:

Essential Cookies

Our website does not use essential cookies. No login or essential functionality requires cookie storage.

Third-Party Cookies

The following third-party services may set cookies when you use our site:

  • Google Fonts: Sets fonts for better typography. Learn more
  • Leaflet.js (CartoDB): Powers our food map. CartoDB tiles may use cookies for map functionality.
  • Instagram/Meta: Embedded Instagram posts may set cookies. Learn more

Newsletter Form

Our newsletter subscription form is handled by Formspree. We do not set our own cookies for this service.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Allow cookies only from websites you trust
  • Block third-party cookies

Note: Blocking cookies may affect some website functionality.
