Who we are

The story behind EAT THIS.

One dish. One restaurant. No compromises.

EAT THIS started in 2024 as a simple idea: instead of listing 50 mediocre restaurants, we pick the one dish you absolutely have to try at each place. No rankings. No top 10 lists. Just honest recommendations from people who actually eat there.

We're a small team based in Berlin. We've been eating our way through the city for years — from hidden ramen bars to Michelin-starred restaurants. We know where the burger hits hardest, where the ramen blows your mind, and where to find the croissant that changes your life.

How we choose

We test every dish personally. We talk to chefs, owners, and regulars. We visit places multiple times, at different hours, on different days. And then we recommend only what we'd order again ourselves.

No paid placements. No sponsored content. If we recommend it, we mean it.

What's next

Berlin is just the beginning. We have our eyes on Istanbul, Amsterdam, Paris — and eventually the whole world. Great food isn't random. It deserves recommendations that actually mean something.
