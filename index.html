Who we are

One dish. One restaurant. No compromises.

EAT THIS started in 2024 as a simple idea: instead of listing 50 mediocre restaurants, we pick the one dish you absolutely have to try at each place. No rankings. No top 10 lists. Just honest recommendations from people who actually eat there.

We're a small team based in Berlin. We've been eating our way through the city for years — from hidden ramen bars to Michelin-starred restaurants.

How we choose

We test every dish personally. We talk to chefs, owners, and regulars. We visit places multiple times. And then we recommend only what we'd order again ourselves.

No paid placements. No sponsored content. If we recommend it, we mean it.

What's next

Berlin is just the beginning. We have our eyes on Istanbul, Amsterdam, Paris — and eventually the whole world.

Was sind Cookies?

Cookies sind kleine Textdateien, die beim Besuch einer Website auf deinem Gerät gespeichert werden. Sie helfen, deine Einstellungen zu speichern und das Surferlebnis zu verbessern.

Wie wir Cookies nutzen
