The 10 Best Donut Shops in Berlin — From Brooklyn Style to Thai

WhatsApp Twitter / X

Berlin has experienced a donut revolution over the past three years. From 2 shops to over 30 — and the quality is better than ever. We tried all 20 and picked the best ten.

1. Atelier Dough — Prenzlauer Berg

The undisputed number one. The donuts here are hand-crafted, the recipes updated monthly. The matcha donut is legendary, the salted caramel donut is poetry. Tip: come in the morning — afternoons often sell out.

2. Momo Mochi Donut — Prenzlauer Berg

Mochi donuts — made from glutinous rice flour — are the hot trend. Momo makes the best in Berlin: chewy, sweet but not heavy. The ube variant (purple sweet potato) is an Instagram hit.

3. Brammibal's — Neukölln

Vegan donuts that nobody would call vegan. The texture is perfect, the glazes creative. The lemon poppy seed is our favourite.

4. The Dough Club — Mitte

Brooklyn-style donuts in Berlin: large, generous, decadent. Fillings range from pistachio cream to Biscoff. Not for every day, but perfect for special occasions.

5–10

The remaining six on our list: Holy Sugar, Da Capo, Jones Ice Cream (yes, they have donuts too), Zeit für Brot, Roamers Coffee and Five Elephant. Find the details in our full guide.
