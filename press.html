About EAT THIS

EAT THIS is a curated food guide for Berlin. Instead of endless lists, we pick the one dish you absolutely have to try at each restaurant. Founded in 2024, we've become the go-to source for honest, no-nonsense food recommendations in Berlin.

Key facts

  • Founded: 2024
  • Location: Berlin, Germany
  • Focus: Curated restaurant recommendations
  • Approach: One dish per restaurant, tested personally
